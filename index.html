ABOUT US

Your Partner in Safety and Compliance

Bright Industrial Consultancy Service specializes in certified non-destructive testing (NDT), third-party inspection, and skilled manpower solutions. We deliver essential services like UT, RT, MPI, DPI, and heat treatment with a focus on safety and compliance.
