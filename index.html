About Us

Bright Industrial Consultancy Service is a trusted provider of industrial inspection and manpower services. With years of experience, we specialize in Non-Destructive Testing (NDT), Third-Party Inspections (TPI), and advanced techniques including EMAT, PAUT, and TOFD. We also deliver drone-based visual inspections and certified manpower solutions for shutdowns and critical maintenance across India.

Our Core Services

NDT (Non-Destructive Testing)

Comprehensive testing methods including UT, RT, MPT, DPT, and VT to detect flaws without damaging materials.

Third-Party Inspection (TPI)

Independent quality audits to ensure compliance with national and international standards.

Certified Manpower

Skilled and certified technicians, engineers, and QA/QC experts deployed for inspection and shutdowns.

EMAT & Advanced NDT

Specialized techniques like EMAT, PAUT, and TOFD for critical assessment in harsh environments.
